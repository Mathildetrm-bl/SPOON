About us

Since 2016, we accompany your daily life so that each meal is a healthy, gourmet and convivial break. We cook fresh products and revisit traditional recipes, for a homemade meal with a touch of fantasy.

After Merlin, the owner of SPOON, dropped out of his accounting studies, he turned to what he had always wanted to do: Catering. He remembered the good times spent with his grandmother in front of a nice hot bowl of soup. He realized that today's fast food is not very healthy or very heart-warming and especially that Paris lacked fast soup food! And that's how SPOON was born!

Our concept

We offer, like other fast food brands, the possibility of choosing a soup base from several choices and then adding ingredients according to your preferences, your mood and your appetite for the day!

We try to offer as many choices as possible that are good for our planet, your health and above all your wallet!
